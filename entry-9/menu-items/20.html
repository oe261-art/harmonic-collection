Sticky Lotus Leaf Rice

.

A classic and popular dim sum dish consisting of glutinous sticky rice filled with various savory ingredients, wrapped in a dried lotus leaf, and then steamed. The leaf imparts a unique, earthy, and aromatic flavor to the rice during steaming.
