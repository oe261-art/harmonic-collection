Chow Fun (beef/chicken)

.

A Cantonese dish featuring wide, flat rice noodles that are stir-fried with other ingredients like beef (of chicken), vegetables, and a savory sauce. Key components often include marinated beef and bean sprouts.

I. Love, This. Shit. Yum, yum, yum, yum, yum. My family and I order this all the time whenever we go out to local-ish Chinese restaurants. One of my earliest food memories is my Mah-Mah feed me as a toddler and getting the noodles and beansprouts all over my clothes and hair. Yeah... Mom wasn't very pleased.
