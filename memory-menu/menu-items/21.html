Fried Chow Mein

.

A popular Chinese dish of stir-fried or pan-fried noodles with vegetables and often meat or tofu. The noodles are fried to achieve a desirable crispy or semi-crispy texture, which are amazing with a simple gravy.

This is a dish I always liked but grew to like even more. It's probably the only fried food my dad is okay with my family and I eating (he's been really into eating healthy). The only thing I never liked about this dish the mushrooms Mah-Mah would add in the gravy; I'd always pick them out... and still do.
